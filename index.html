About

I am a young scientist with a keen interest in understanding the nature of the universe. In my BSc degree at the Queensland Univeristy of Technology (QUT), I majored in physics and also undertook minors in astrophysics and earth science. I then began my MPhil degree in extragalactic cosmology at QUT, where I studied the physical processes of distant galaxy environments and evolution. Throughout my MPhil degree, I also had the pleasure of engaging in media and outreach activities and collaborating with supervisors in external universities in conjuction to the exciting research I undertook. I now hope to continue my passion for astrophysics research, outreach and teaching in my PhD degree.
